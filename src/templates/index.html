Depression is Real! You are Not Alone.

Depression has become a significant mayhem plaguing the world today. About 265 million individuals of all ages suffer from depression worldwide. Of these, about 75% remain untreated, with one million individuals taking their lives every year. Thus, depression is amongst the leading causes of suicide esp. amongst adolescents. Hence, the time has come to use the reach of social media to combat this havoc.
